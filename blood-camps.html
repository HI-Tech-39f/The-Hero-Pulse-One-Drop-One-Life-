Blood Camps / Blood Donation Drives

Organizing blood donation camps is an effective way to meet the increasing demand of blood. Every year, blood camps are organized on 14th June in all units of Institute of Blood Transfusion Service.

In these camps, free blood grouping and screening facilities are provided to blood donors. The collected blood is issued to thalassemia, cancer and other needy patients on Non Donor Basis (NDB).

Blood donation camps help save thousands of lives. Due to the increase in surgeries and medical emergencies, regular blood donation drives are essential to meet the rising demand.

Blood donation camps are organized to promote voluntary blood donation and ensure availability of safe blood.
