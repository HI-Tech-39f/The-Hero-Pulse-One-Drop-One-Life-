Frequently Asked Questions (FAQS)

Can I donate if I recently had a cold?

You should be completely symptom-free for at least 48 hours before donating blood.

How long is the recovery?

Most people feel normal immediately. Your body replaces the fluid volume within 24 hours.

Is the equipment sterile?

Yes, we use brand new, sterile, disposable needles for every single donor.

Do I get results of my blood test?

Yes, we notify donors if any abnormalities are found during the routine screening of their blood.
